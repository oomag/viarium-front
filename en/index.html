What is Viarium?

VIARIUM is a tool that will completely replace physical space. VIARIUM consists of a single virtual space that includes VR worlds and VR architecture of shopping centers, separate buildings, showrooms and other VR models created by the VIARIUM platform.

The platform is intended for the widest audience. Participants and users of the project have a unique opportunity to create and develop their own sales outlets and entire virtual worlds. In addition to boosting sales, they get the opportunity for securing additional income.

Business Users Developers Advertising

Why blockchain?

Blockchain technology in the VIARIUM project enables such features as: registration of rights to real estate property in a distributed ledger, secure, transparent and fast transactions for the sale of virtual property with funds sent through a smart contract, transparent and fair voting on various issues of the community: use of funds, blocking members with inappropriate content upon request from other members of the system, creating new territories etc.

In the future the use of blockchain will reduce the cost of server capacity by distributing the network load to multiple participating nodes.

F.A.Q.
Viarium is the first decentralized VR-platform to conduct business on blockchain, a convenient and easily accessible environment in which companies and their consumers can interact. The use of blockchain technology enables us to authenticate ownership rights to a range of digital items available on the platform such as land plots, shopping areas, VR-worlds. VR technology creates the distinct sensation of presence: one may walk around an apartment, a residential complex or even a residential area that are yet to be built; test-drive a car without leaving the comfort of one's home or experience an object of digital art in a new dimension. We have already implemented the following commercial projects: a virtual realtor for one of Russia's largest banks, a VR shop for one of the largest online retailers, and an animated contemporary art gallery for a talented artist.
